# NOT an inference engine on this hardware: the cu128 builds target sm_75+ and
# cannot launch kernels on the Quadro P5000. torch is pinned solely for the
# cuBLAS/cuDNN binaries in torch/lib that ctranslate2 loads (see cuda_dlls.py).
# Keep the +cu128 local version AND the extra index: a bare `torch>=X` resolves a
# newer CPU-only wheel from PyPI and silently drops those DLLs.
# 2.11.0+cu128 is the newest build available on the cu128 index.
--extra-index-url https://download.pytorch.org/whl/cu128
torch==2.11.0+cu128
